About the Data Explorer

An interactive web application designed to explore some of the metrics coming out of Hollywood. The tool allows exploration of over 500 films from the past 5 years.

  • Hover over a bubble to see the film details.
  • Click on a bubble to highlight it. Click 'clear' beside the searchbox to clear the selection.
  • Use the Chart controls on the right to play with the data.

Does the critical rating of a film affect its profitability? Set the X-Axis to RT Rating, the Y-Axis to Profit to find out. There are hundreds of possible combinations available with all of the controls.

WebKit seems to handle the SVG animations pretty well. Try Chrome or Safari if the app seems slow or unresponsive.

Data provided by Information is Beautiful. Big thanks to d3.js (@mbostock) for the amazing library.
